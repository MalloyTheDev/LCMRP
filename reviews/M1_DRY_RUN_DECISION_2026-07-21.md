## Final-judge findings

1. **Contract completeness:** Each bundle has one isolated active subject, one frozen study, exactly one published atomic record per planned analysis, and one published closeout whose unique analysis ledger is set-equal to the frozen plan.
2. **Immutable binding:** Bundle-native and repository tests recompute raw-byte SHA-256 values and bind exact subject, method profile, manifest, analysis, finding, closeout, and local-index targets.
3. **Failure behavior:** Representative digest, identity, profile, lifecycle, ledger-cardinality, registry-contamination, and false-claim mutations are rejected.
4. **Evidence boundary:** Synthetic identifiers and repeated non-evidentiary notices distinguish fixture lifecycle words from real research. No finding awards or changes a mechanism evidence label.
5. **Registry isolation:** The local example indexes are rooted inside their bundles. All seven production registries remain empty.
6. **Product independence:** Neither bundle selects a model, embedding provider, storage engine, vector database, application schema, continuous service, user interface, or memory implementation.

## Accepted limitations

- The taxonomy cases, expected classifications, and serializer were constructed together. There is no representative sample, independent adjudication, semantic-validity result, biological-validity result, or external-utility result.
- The taxonomy bundle's `COMPLETED` row records deterministic serialization only; its claim assessment is `INCONCLUSIVE`. The separate adjudication analysis is explicitly `NOT_RUN`.
- The formal system is deliberately trivial, manually encoded, and checked by a local four-valuation replay. It neither models nor tests FMO-0.1 and is not an independently implemented proof.
- Schema validity and exact digests do not prove truth, authorship, provenance authenticity, novelty, scientific merit, or resistance to consistent authorized replacement.
- Mutation coverage is representative, not exhaustive. Future schema or packaging changes can create untested bypasses or false rejections.
- No external registrar or reviewer reproduced the freeze, record graph, semantic replay, or case handling.
